# Stellar Dominion - Quick Start Guide

**Game Version:** 1.0.0-release  
**Status:** ✅ Production Ready

---

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### 1. Access the Game
- **URL:** [stellar-dominion.replit.dev](https://stellar-dominion.replit.dev)
- **Port:** 5000 (locFirebase Studio> ✘ Preview failed to startal development)
- Open in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

### 2. Create Your Account
- Click **"Register"** on the login screen
- Enter a username and password
- Your empire is created automatically
- You'll be taken to the **Empire Setup** screen

### 3. Complete Empire Setup
- Choose your empire name and settings
- Select your difficulty level
- Customize starting resources
- Click **"Begin!"** to start your adventure

### 4. Explore the Dashboard
Once logged in, you'll see the **Overview** page with:
- 💰 Resources (Metal, Crystal, Deuterium)
- 🏗️ Facilities (buildings you can construct)
- 🚀 Fleet (ships in your command)
- 📊 Empire Level & Tier progression
- 💵 Currency (Silver, Gold, Platinum)

## 🎮 Core Game Systems

### 📊 **Empire Progression**
- Level: 1-999 (earn experience to level up)
- Tier: 1-21 (unlock bonuses and new buildings)
- Prestige: Hard reset with permanent multipliers
- **Page:** Empire Progression

### 💰 **Resources & Economy**
- **Metal** (1x value) - Basic construction
- **Crystal** (1.5x value) - Tech components
- **Deuterium** (2x value) - Fuel & energy
- Buy/sell on the Market
- **Page:** Resources, Market

### 🏦 **Bank System**
- Deposit currency for safekeeping
- Earn interest on deposits
- View transaction history
- Track your wealth
- **Page:** Settings → Bank

### 💳 **3-Tier Currency**
- **Silver** (everyday transactions)
- **Gold** (premium features, 100x value)
- **Platinum** (exclusive items, 10,000x value)
- Convert between currencies
- **Page:** Resources → Currency

### 🧠 **Knowledge Library**
- Master 10 knowledge types (Military, Engineering, Science, etc.)
- 4 classes × 5 tiers = 200+ progression levels
- 2000+ mastery points across all systems
- Unlock synergy bonuses
- **Page:** Knowledge Library

### ⚔️ **Combat System**
- 5 battle formations (Balanced, Aggressive, Defensive, Flanking, Pincer)
- Multi-layer damage (Shield, Armor, Hull)
- Fleet vs Fleet combat
- Detailed battle logs
- **Page:** Combat, Battle Logs

### 🏗️ **Facilities (120+)**
- **Resource:** Metal Mine, Crystal Mine, Refinery
- **Energy:** Solar Power Plant, Nuclear Reactor
- **Storage:** Resource Storage, Crystal Vault
- **Military:** Barracks, Shipyard, Weapons Factory
- **Research:** Laboratory, Tech Center
- **Page:** Facilities, Tech Tree

### 🚀 **Expeditions**
- Send fleets on 5 expedition types
- Exploration, Military, Scientific, Trade, Conquest
- Encounter management & rewards
- **Page:** Expeditions

### 🤝 **Alliances**
- Form alliances with other players
- Trade resources
- Mutual defense pacts
- Raid groups
- **Page:** Alliance

### 📈 **Rankings & Leaderboard**
- Empire Value calculated from resources + currency + fleet
- Top 100 empires ranked
- Real-time updates
- **Page:** Empire Progression

## 🎯 Early Game Tips (First Hour)

### Turn 1-5
1. Build basic **Metal Mines** (+2-3 levels)
2. Start researching **Metal Extraction**
3. Build **Crystal Mines** once you have resources
4. Produce basic **Civilian Units**

### Turn 6-20
1. Build **Refineries** to process resources
2. Research **Crystal Processing**
3. Build **Shipyard** to construct ships
4. Create first **Fleet** with 10-20 ships

### Turn 21-50
1. Build **Energy Facilities** (Solar Power Plants)
2. Research **Armor** & **Weapons** tech
3. Construct **Military Units** (Warriors, Rangers)
4. Join an **Alliance** for mutual defense
5. Explore nearby star systems

### Continuous
- Visit **Bank** to deposit excess currency
- Check **Market** for profitable trades
- Increase **Knowledge** mastery
- Level your **Empire**
- Complete **Achievements** for rewards

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Can't Login"
- Check your username/password
- Verify cookies are enabled
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### "Server Not Responding"
- Check your internet connection
- Reload the page
- Wait 30 seconds and try again
- Check server status page

### "Resources Not Updating"
- Refresh the page (F5)
- Close and reopen the application
- Check that your buildings are producing
- Verify research queues are running

### "Performance Issues"
- Reduce UI quality in settings
- Enable performance mode
- Close other browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
